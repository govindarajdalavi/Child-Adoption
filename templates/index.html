Adoption Workflow

Legal Adoption Process

1. Registration

Prospective adoptive parents register and upload identity, income, and medical proofs.

2. Home Study

Licensed social worker conducts physical home inspection to verify family suitability.

3. Court Orders

District Family Court issues Temporary Foster Care Order, followed by Final Adoption Order.

4. Post-Adoption

Social worker conducts 3 mandatory follow-up visits over 2 years (Regulation 13).

Strict Government Oversight & Privacy Protection

Our portal is designed exclusively for Dharwad District administrative use, connecting all critical stakeholders in the adoption lifecycle.

  • CWC Validated Orphan Database

    Every child is legally declared free for adoption by the Child Welfare Committee before being visible to parents.

  • Real-time Court Integration

    Direct workflow integration with the District Family Court to eliminate illegal handovers.

  • Child Privacy Protocol

    Strict prevention of unauthorized photo scraping. Images are protected and displayed securely only to verified parents.

Collector & Admin Oversight

Complete macroscopic view of all district adoption cases with interactive analytics and bottleneck detection.

Field Social Workers
